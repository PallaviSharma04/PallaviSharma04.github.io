About me

Numbers have always fascinated me, not just as figures on a screen, but as puzzles waiting to be solved. I love the thrill of querying problems, breaking them down, and piecing together solutions that makes sense of the overwhelming chaos.

My journey began with engineering, took a detour into government service. But life had other plans for me, I moved to Mumbai and got into private tutorship. During this time, I explored different fields in pursuit of genuine fulfillment- that's when I discovered my passion for data analysis- a space where I lose track of time and have found my true calling.

With a sharp analytical mindset, natural fluency in data and logic, and an unwavering drive to continuously learn and improve, I specialize in transforming raw datasets into meaningful stories. I believe every dataset has a story- I just happen to speak its language.
